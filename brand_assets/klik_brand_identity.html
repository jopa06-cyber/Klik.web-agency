Brand usage rules

Always use the period. "Klik." not "Klik" — the full stop is part of the mark and signals confidence.
Ember red is an accent, not a fill. Use it on the dot, the icon mark, and key CTAs only — never flood a page with it.
Chalk background only. Never use pure white (#FFF) — always the warm off-white #F6F5F3 for surfaces.
Serif for headlines, Inter for everything else. Never mix more than two typefaces in one layout.
